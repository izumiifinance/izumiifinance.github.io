izumi finance – A Beginner’s Guide

iZUMi Finance is a multi-chain DeFi protocol providing One-Stop Liquidity as a Service (LaaS). Its philosophy is that every token deserves better on-chain liquidity in an efficient and lasting way. As our name “iZUMi” in Japanese signifies, our ultimate goal is to help every partner and token to enjoy on-chain liquidity like spring water, continuous and long-lasting for centuries.

iZUMi has debuted LiquidBox, iZiSwap, and iUSD three on-chain liquidity products. LiquidBox provides Programmable Liquidity as a Service (LaaS) firstly on Ethereum with Uniswap V3. With the deployment of Uniswap V3 in other Ethereum Layer 2 ecosystems, LiquidBox was also successfully deployed on Arbitrum & Polygon. iZUMi Finance then launched iZiswap on BNB Chain with the innovative Discretized-Liquidity-AMM(DL-AMM) model, which was upgraded from Uniswap V3's concentrated liquidity model to implement an AMM mechanism that supports discrete liquidity on every price tick, providing DEX users with a CEX-like trading experience to place limit orders in a non-custodial orderbook way. iZUMi has also successfully issued iZUMi Bond USD (iUSD) and iZUMi Bond, and supported Bond Farming which brings stable fixed income to on-chain investors, providing a high yield level fixed income investment product for users and combining with the TradFi product model of convertible bonds to provide iZUMi Bond investors the Option which can convert to iZi token.

iZUMi will continue to expand the multi-chain ecology and eventually support cross-chain liquidity trading mechanisms, bring systematic liquidity service to the multi-chain ecosystem, and eventually become a one-stop liquidity service platform with super high capital efficiency in the DeFi industry.

LiquidBox

iZUMi’s LiquidBox enhances incentive efficiency, enabling protocols to distribute incentive rewards in certain price ranges. LiquidBox will help liquidity providers earn additional liquidity mining rewards provided by related protocols as well as DEX trading fees. On the other side, LiquidBox solves “pool 2 dilemmas” problems by providing structured-incentive and auto-rebased modules, which could attract more liquidity with a low emission rate for protocols while enabling non-impermanent loss liquidity mining for liquidity providers. This innovative Liquidity Mining mechanism helps protocols attract liquidity in an efficient and lasting way.

Concentrated Liquidity
The concept of concentrated liquidity is central to iZUMi Finance's offerings. Unlike traditional liquidity pools that spread assets across a broad price range, iZUMi Finance allows liquidity providers to concentrate their assets within specific price bands. This approach maximizes capital efficiency, as assets are utilized where they are most needed, leading to better returns for liquidity providers and improved trading conditions for users.
